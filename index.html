Privacy Policy 🔒

Last updated: 2025 | NS_.NAILZ by Nandni Soni

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, WhatsApp number, and optional city. This data is stored locally in your browser and is never shared with third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information personalizes your experience and allows you to place orders via WhatsApp. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data.

3. Data Storage & Security

All account data is stored in your browser's local storage. We do not operate external servers or databases. Clearing your browser data will remove your account information.

4. Order Privacy

When you click "Order Now," you are redirected to WhatsApp. Your name is shared in the message for personalization. Your WhatsApp conversation is governed by WhatsApp's privacy policy.

5. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Session data is managed via browser storage for login functionality only.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Your Rights

You may delete your account at any time by clearing your browser's local storage. You may contact us to request information about your data.

8. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us via WhatsApp at +91 7285058133.
